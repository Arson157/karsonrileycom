Welcome to House of Carbs!

Address: 25 W High St, Oxford, OH 45056

Phone: (513) 524-3114

Hours:

Mon-Thurs: 5-10 | Fri-Sat: 6-12 | Sun: Closed

We are an Italian restaurant located in Oxford, OH just outside of Miami University’s campus. We started in 2015 when a group of students wanted to eat some fine Italian cuisine at a reasonable price. These students rallied together to create the restaurant and make it a place that the students of Miami would want to come eat at on the reg. These students enlisted the help of Miami’s culinary department to creating a menu, Farmer School of Business’ marketing department to create advertisements to promote House of Carbs, and the interior design division to create a desirable atmosphere, and most importantly create an awesome website.

House of Carbs has similar Italian food as Paesano’s but with much more reasonable pricing. Students have come to us and said that we are their favorite restaurant because it provides a nice break from the same old campus food and they mention that you cannot get italian food other than greasy food court pizza and cheap pasta. House of Carbs has the best of both worlds: good food at a good price.

Enjoy!
