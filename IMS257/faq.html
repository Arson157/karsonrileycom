Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I have dietary restrictions?

We have options for many different diets, if you have certain dietary restrictions, let your server know, and we will accommodate you accordingly.

What are your hours?

We are open from 5pm-10pm Sunday through Thursday, and 5pm-12pm Friday and Saturday.

Do you take reservations?

We do take reservations. Call us up to 2 hours in advance to secure a table for you and your party.

Where are you located?

We are located in Oxford OH, on High Street right beside Fiesta Charra.
